Fishy Stuff

Fishing for Money

by Karpfen

Stand around AFK guide

Good Money, Bad EXP Edition

Prerequisites

Fishing level 5

Character profile -> Ability -> Fishing

  • Guild Skill Art of Fishing
  • Fishing Clothes (Silver Embroidered/Manos Fisher’s Clothes)
  • Food like Marvelous Balacs Lunchbox

Max Auto-fishing reduction (67%)

See my Fishing Time Calculator Different combinations possible, but easy mode TL;DR is

  • Balenos Fishing Rod +10
  • T4/T5 Pet with Special: Auto-Fishing Time Reduction (Otter, Penguin, …)
  • Lighstone Set: Nibbles

More Equipment

  • Shim Cheong’s Miraculous Fish Tank
  • Maple Float (+rare %)
  • Lightstone Set: Sharp-eyed Seagull (+rare %)
  • Archaeologist’s Map (saves you 5mil each time you use Magnus to sell)

Mastery

Aim for decent mastery (>1500)

  • High fishing lifeskill level
  • Manos accessories/Clothes

Other stuff

  • High fishing lifeskill level for max Durability Reduction Resistance (DRR caps out at Guru 20)
  • Professional Trader’s Clothes (or costume version) let’s you haggle more times, saving some energy and your patience

How to stand around AFK

  • Fish in Velia/Coastal Cliff -> see Location. Make sure fish origin is Coastal Cliff!
  • Archmap to Velia
  • Go to Magnus at Bartali Farm and teleport to Nampo Moodle Village
  • Optionally: Bukpo for extra distance
  • Channel hop until you get decent prices on prizefish (120-130%) or you get bored
  • Sell fish
  • Teleport back using Archmap
