Contribute

We welcome you to contribute! Contributions can be as simple as comments/bug reports, feedback of any kind, ideas or even full new guides.

If you want to contribute, you may want to

  • Join the communities on discord and let us know!
  • Shoot me a direct message on discord (karpfen) or even ingame (Carp, EU server)
  • Submit a Merge-Request to the repository on GitHub.

Don’t worry, no coding skills are needed! Knowing Markdown is enough.
