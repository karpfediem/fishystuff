Contribute

We welcome you to contribute! Contributions can be as simple as comments/bug reports, feedback of any kind, ideas or even full new guides.

If you want to contribute, you may want to

  1. Submit a Merge-Request to the repository on GitHub
  2. Join the communities on discord and let us know!
